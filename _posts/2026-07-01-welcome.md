First post. This blog is where I'll drop write-ups on offensive security,
threat intelligence, and incident response.

## What to expect

- **Pentest notes** — recon-to-report, with real PoCs and concrete impact.
- **Threat intel** — actor tracking and IOC analysis, defensive focus.
- **IR** — post-compromise forensics and playbook refinement.

Posts live in `_posts/` as Markdown. New post = new file named
`YYYY-MM-DD-title.md` with front matter up top.

cat > _posts/2026-07-15-my-topic.md <<'EOF'
---
layout: post
title: "My Topic"
date: 2026-07-15 12:00:00 +0200
---

body here
EOF
